Phase 1
Consolidate & Connect
~12 weeks
Weeks 1–212-Bar Foundation
Complete Blues Guitar (Book 1, Ch. 1–2). Learn the basic 12-bar structure and open string riffs. This is the framework underlying nearly everything on your song list. Parallel: Purple Haze rhythm parts and Paranoid riff to full song.
Weeks 3–4Dominant 7 Chords & Extensions
Complete Blues Guitar (Book 1, Ch. 3–4). First chord vocabulary upgrade — from power chords to dominant 7 colors. Parallel: Sunshine of Your Love rhythm and intro, continue Purple Haze.
Weeks 5–6Turnarounds & Triplet Feel
Complete Blues Guitar (Book 1, Ch. 6–7). Turnarounds are the glue of blues structure. The triplet feel chapter will immediately change how your rhythm sits in the groove. Parallel: Back in Black full song, Hey Joe rhythm parts.
Weeks 7–8Soloing Basics, Bending & Vibrato
Complete Blues Guitar (Book 2, Ch. 1 + Appendices A & B). The bending appendix alone is worth the price of the book — intentional, in-tune bending separates blues phrasing from noodling. Parallel: Purple Haze solo, Paranoid solo, Back in Black solo.
Weeks 9–10Melodic Fills & Rhythmic Divisions
Complete Blues Guitar (Book 1, Ch. 9) + Complete Blues Guitar (Book 2, Ch. 2). Fills chapter is your first proper phrasing vocabulary builder. Parallel: Hey Joe solo, Sunshine of Your Love solo, Wish You Were Here full song.
Weeks 11–12Consolidation & Milestone Assessment
No new book material. Full run-throughs of all six songs. Record yourself playing each one. Hearing yourself back is the most efficient diagnostic tool there is.
Phase 2
Blues Vocabulary
~14 weeks
Weeks 1–2Minor Blues & the BB Box
Complete Blues Guitar (Book 1, Ch. 13) + Complete Blues Guitar (Book 2, Ch. 5). Parallel: The Thrill Is Gone full song, begin Babe I'm Gonna Leave You.
Weeks 3–4Rhythmic Displacement & Call & Response
Complete Blues Guitar (Book 2, Ch. 3–4). Parallel: finish The Thrill Is Gone, Babe I'm Gonna Leave You full arrangement.
Weeks 5–6Connecting Pentatonic Positions
Complete Blues Guitar (Book 2, Ch. 5) continued. Parallel: Crossroads rhythm and lead, Whole Lotta Love riff.
Weeks 7–8Blues at Rock Tempo
Crossroads and Whole Lotta Love up to tempo. Parallel: both songs full, including solos.
Weeks 9–10Riff Architecture & Groove
Complete Blues Guitar (Book 1, Ch. 10–11). Parallel: La Grange full song, begin Heartbreaker.
Weeks 11–12Playing the Changes — Guide Tones
Complete Blues Guitar (Book 3, Part 1, Ch. 1–2). Parallel: Heartbreaker full song, begin Layla.
Weeks 13–14Capstone — Layla
Complete Blues Guitar (Book 3, Part 1, Ch. 3–4). Full run-throughs of all seven songs.
Phase 3
Modal Depth & the North Star
~15 weeks
Weeks 1–2Slow Blues Depth
Complete Blues Guitar (Book 3, Part 2, Ch. 6). Mixolydian enters here. Parallel: Since I've Been Loving You, slow and deliberate.
Weeks 3–4Dark Modal Vocabulary
Heavy Metal Bible (Ch. 4) — Natural Minor/Aeolian. Parallel: Black Sabbath full arrangement, begin War Pigs riff work.
Weeks 5–6Riff Architecture — Iommi
Heavy Metal Bible (Ch. 6 — Riff Writing). Parallel: War Pigs full song, Iron Man full song.
Weeks 7–8NWOBHM Picking — The Trooper
Heavy Metal Bible (Ch. 3 — Developing the Picking Hand). Parallel: The Trooper full song.
Weeks 9–10Sustained Melodic Phrasing
Complete Blues Guitar (Book 3, Part 2, Ch. 8 & 9). Week 9: solo one. Week 10: solo two.
Weeks 11–12Hendrix Chord Voicings — Little Wing Groundwork
Dedicated preparation block. Thumb-over-the-neck voicings and chord-melody concept.
Weeks 13–15Little Wing — Capstone
Week 13: intro and verse chord-melody at slow tempo. Week 14: the solo. Week 15: full run-throughs. Record yourself.
